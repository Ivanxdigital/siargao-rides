Frequently Asked Questions

Do you track flight delays?

Yes. We ask for your flight number upon booking. Our drivers monitor arrivals, so if your flight is delayed, we adjust our pickup time at no extra charge.

Can you drop us off at any accommodation?

Absolutely. We provide door-to-door service to resorts, villas, and homestays in General Luna, Cloud 9, Pacifico, and other areas (rates may vary for remote locations).

Is fuel included in the full-day hire?

Yes, our full-day private van hire includes the vehicle, a professional local driver, and fuel for standard island routes.

How do we pay?

Payment details are discussed and finalized via WhatsApp during the booking process. We accept cash on arrival or local bank transfers (GCash/Maya).
